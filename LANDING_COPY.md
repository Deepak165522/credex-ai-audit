# FAQ

## 1. How does the AI Spend Audit work?

The platform analyzes your current AI tooling subscriptions, estimated usage patterns, and monthly spending to identify potential savings opportunities and unnecessary costs.

---

## 2. Does the platform require login or payment?

No. The audit is completely free and does not require account creation before viewing results.

---

## 3. Are the recommendations AI-generated?

The financial calculations themselves are deterministic and rule-based for accuracy. AI is used only to generate personalized natural-language summaries.

---

## 4. Can startups really reduce AI costs significantly?

In many cases, yes. Early-stage teams often overpay for enterprise plans or duplicate tooling that exceeds their actual requirements.

---

## 5. What happens after I save my report?

Users may optionally receive follow-up optimization recommendations and information about discounted AI infrastructure credits through Credex.

# Tone and Messaging Goals

The copy was designed to feel:

* Technical but accessible
* Founder-focused
* Practical instead of overly promotional
* Modern SaaS-oriented
* Financially credible

The overall positioning aims to make the platform feel like a useful operational tool rather than a marketing gimmick.
