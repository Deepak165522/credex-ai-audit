# User Interview 1

## Participant

* Name: Bharat Bhushan
* Role: Computer Science Student / Project Builder
* Company Stage: Individual / Student Projects

---

## Key Quotes

> “I mostly use ChatGPT because the student version gives really fast replies.”

> “The unlimited image generation feature is very useful for my projects.”

> “AI tools help me complete project work much faster.”

---

## Current AI Tooling Usage

* Tools used: ChatGPT
* Estimated monthly spend: Primarily student/free access
* Team size: Individual user

---

## Most Surprising Insight

The participant valued speed and convenience significantly more than advanced enterprise-level features. Cost optimization mattered less than quick responses and productivity improvements.

---

## What Changed In My Product Thinking

This interview highlighted that not every user is focused on reducing costs aggressively. Some users prioritize:

* Faster workflows
* Convenience
* Access to premium AI features

This reinforced the importance of avoiding overly aggressive downgrade recommendations in the audit engine for users who genuinely benefit from premium capabilities.

The participant cared more about image quality and quick idea generation than advanced coding-specific AI features. Reliability issues were tolerated as long as the tool remained useful overall.

This interview showed that many users evaluate AI tools based on:

* Content quality
* Ease of use

This reinforced the importance of balancing cost optimization recommendations with actual user workflow preferences.

# User Interview 3

## Participant

* Name: Jotish Yadav
* Role: Cybersecurity Student
* Company Stage: Individual / Learning Projects

---

## Key Quotes

> “I initially used ChatGPT and Gemini, but sometimes the answers were inaccurate during quizzes and technical tasks.”

> “Now I mostly use Claude AI because the responses feel more accurate.”

> “Claude gives cleaner code examples, especially for cybersecurity-related work.”

---

## Current AI Tooling Usage

* Tools used: Claude AI, ChatGPT, Gemini
* Estimated monthly spend: Low-cost / student usage
* Team size: Individual user

---

## Most Surprising Insight

The participant prioritized answer accuracy much more heavily than feature count or interface design. Trust in technical correctness directly influenced tool preference.

---

## What Changed In My Product Thinking

This interview reinforced that users choose AI tools for very different reasons depending on their workflows.

For technical and cybersecurity-related tasks, users cared most about:

* Accurate responses
* Reliable code generation
* Fewer hallucinations
* Better technical explanations

This influenced the recommendation engine by highlighting that optimization suggestions should not focus only on pricing, but also on whether a tool genuinely fits the user’s use case and trust requirements.

---

# Common Patterns Across Interviews

Across all three interviews, several consistent themes appeared:

* Users value productivity improvements more than purely reducing costs
* Accuracy and response quality strongly influence tool selection
* Different workflows require different AI tools
* Users tolerate occasional AI mistakes if the overall experience remains useful
* Trust and practical usefulness matter more than marketing claims

---

# Key Learnings

The interviews validated that users often subscribe to AI tools without fully evaluating whether their plans match their actual needs.

The conversations also showed that:

* Users care about both cost and workflow efficiency
* Recommendations must feel practical and trustworthy
* Different user types prioritize different AI capabilities
* Financial optimization should never compromise genuine productivity needs
